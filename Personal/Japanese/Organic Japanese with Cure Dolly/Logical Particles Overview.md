
Logical particles describe the roles of nouns in a sentence and how they connect to the verb. They answer key questions like:

- **Who did it?**
    
- **What was affected?**
    
- **Where?**
    
- **In what direction?**
    
- **With what?**
    

These particles **always attach to nouns** and form essential sentence structure.
